About Us

DLSU-PUSA (Professors for the Uplifttment of Society's Animals) is an organization that cares mainly for the Taft campus' cats. We feed, neuter, and spay to control the cat population. Additionally, we provide vaccination/antirabies shots, medical care, adopting/rehoming of stray cats & occasionally even stray dogs, etc.
